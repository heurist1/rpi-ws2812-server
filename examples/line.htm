In this example we will draw 2 lines of 32 pixels on an 8x32 display.
There are 2 commands to draw lines:

  • draw_line: draws a line with cairo library, with anti aliasing. This may not produce sharp 1 pixel lines, read more
  • draw_sharp_line: draws a line with Bresenham algorithm, no anti aliasing

setup 1,256,3 init brightness 1,32 config_2D 1,32,8,3,32,8 draw_line 1,0,0.5,31.5,0.5,1,FF0000 draw_sharp_line 1,0,6,31,6,1,00FF00 render

NOTE: draw_sharp_line doesn't support alpha transparency and all parameters must be integers.
