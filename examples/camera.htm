You can capture video and paint it directly on your 2D matrix. You need to install the Raspberry Pi camera or USB camera / HDMI capture device that supports v4l2 MJPEG output. This example creates a 500x800 display and captures images from /dev/video0 (default Raspberry Pi camera).

setup 1,400000,3 init config_2D 1,500,800,0,500,800 camera 1,"/dev/video0",0,0,500,800
